Tapestri.org

Websight Redesign

Tapestri.org is a charitable organization specializing in helping victims of human trafficking and Domestic Violence.

UX/UI Designers

Terika Newson

Lauren Novinski

Margarita Polishchuk

Problem Statement

We have observed that Tapestri.org’s navigation isn’t directing the user to topics that they assumed would be addressed. How might we improve the website so that users will be able to better identify the information that they are looking for?

Possible Solution

Through interviews, our team has found that the best solution to our users problem would be to rework the navigation and to regroup the categories. We feel that in this redesign, Tapestri’s users will be better able to find the information they are looking for.
